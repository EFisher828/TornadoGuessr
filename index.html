TornadoGuessr

Test your skills by analyzing random tornado days!

Yes, you could cheat by looking up the date, but where's the fun in that?

Click a point on the map to make a prediction. Concentric circles with 50, 100, and 150 mi radii will appear.

A perfect score will be awarded if all tornado reports lie within a circle with 50 mi radius.

For this particular day, you are allowed up to guesses.
